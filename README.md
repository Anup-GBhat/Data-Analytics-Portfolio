## Project 1: Executive Commercial Operations (Power BI)
<img width="1301" height="740" alt="PowerBI_Dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d3902394-768e-4b0b-8df2-2a8685316aef" />(PowerBI_Dashboard.png)

## **Project Overview** 
Analyzed **$118M in revenue** to identify key profit drivers across international markets. 

## **Key Insights&Features** 
* **Top Margin Driver:** Identified **'Amarilla'** as the highest margin product at **14.78%**.
* **Global Scope:** Visualized performance across 5 countries (USA, Mexico, France, Germany, Canada) using geospatial mapping.
* **Interactivity:** Integrated **Date Slicers** and **Segment Filters** (Government, Enterprise, Small Business) for granular auditing.
* **Technical Stack:** Utilized **Power Query** for ETL and **DAX** for calculating margin percentages and total profits.

## Project 2: AI Labor Risk Executive Summary (Tableau)

!<img width="996" height="793" alt="Tableau_Dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9ded8ae1-0a60-4064-b7db-91a7a8863b25" />(Tableau_Dashboard.png)

### **Project Overview**
This exploratory analysis examines the risk of automation and AI displacement across various professional sectors and education levels using a sample of 2,000 global data points.

### **Key Insights & Features**
* **Workforce Segmentation:** Categorized job roles into three impact levels: Unchanged, Modified, and Replaced.
* **Industry Benchmarking:** Analyzed employee distribution across 7 industries, identifying IT and Manufacturing as high-impact zones.
* **Educational Correlation:** Utilized 100% stacked bar charts to correlate automation risk with education levels, providing a nuanced view of labor vulnerability.
* **Tooling:** Developed using **Tableau Desktop**, featuring advanced calculated fields for percentage-of-total distributions.
